Types of Outdoor Advertising

The most commonly known type is the billboard. The most common forms of outdoor advertising today fall into three categories:

  • Billboard Advertising (Hoarding)
  • Street Furniture — in parks, roadside, etc.
  • Transit — buses, trains, auto, metro, etc.

Why Outdoor Advertising Matters

We spend a lot of our lives outside our homes. Advertisers fighting to stand out amid the clutter of ads on devices need a less-cluttered environment to deliver their message. Outdoor advertising is important because it’s accessible, measurable, effective, and less intrusive than many other forms of advertising.

Digital Outdoor Advertising

Also known as digital signage, digital outdoor advertising uses electronic technology to change what’s displayed on a screen. Digital billboards can feature messages from multiple advertisers that rotate every six to eight seconds. Other digital signs might be free-standing on an urban sidewalk or courtside at a venue. Advertising follows consumers wherever they go—in supermarket carts, sports arenas, health clubs—and can be high-tech (digital) or low-tech (e.g. printed mini-posters). We deliver both traditional and digital outdoor solutions.
