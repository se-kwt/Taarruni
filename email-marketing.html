Email Marketing

What we deliver

The email server and its control panel comes with every Domain. The webmail facility is provided by any site administrator. Each website needs at least one email account (e.g. info@yourcompanyname.com) which is very important to establish your business online and for further business correspondence. We are providing E-mail Marketing Services in India. If you registered with us, we can assist you with all technical aspects of emailing and accessing activities. Full control of all features of your mail solution through easy-to-use, web-based interfaces—fast, reliable, and convenient.

Key Features of Our Email Solutions

  • Customized mailbox quotas and addition of mailboxes as per business growth
  • Secure IMAP and POP support for incoming mail
  • Simple Mail Transfer Protocol (SMTP) support for outgoing mail
  • Spam Analysis Engine with auto-updates
  • Web-based administration with full control of all features

Our Webmail Features

  • Prioritizing messages, mail sorting and filtering
  • Delivery confirmation
  • Integrated address book and calendar
  • User selectable language & theme settings
  • Folders support and multiple attachments
  • Multiple identities & signatures

Benefits for Mass Campaigns

  • Better deliverability: Dedicated service helps avoid spam folders when sending at scale.
  • Performance analytics: Track metrics and click-through rates to measure campaign success.
  • Manage unsubscribes: Contact records update automatically so you don’t resend to opted-out users.
  • Send more emails: Plans that meet your volume vs. ISP daily limits.
  • Design attractive emails: Professional, responsive HTML emails with design tools.

Find the Top Email Marketing Service for Your Business

When comparing email marketing service providers, bear in mind your specific needs: advanced automation and segmentation, a rich selection of editable email templates, and fit for multichannel campaigns. Each service offers different plans and pricing—so it’s worth determining the right platform for the features you need and the number of contacts you plan to reach. The best email marketing service will help you grow in the short term and remain the ultimate solution as your needs develop.
