Domain Registration and Hosting

Taarruni Design Solutions (TDS) is a leading digital marketing and IT solutions company.

What we deliver

Domain name is the unique identity given to your business and company on the web. As no two companies can have the same domain name, it’s critical to decide the name so as to get the relevant views and responses for your business. Every company needs to buy a domain and get it registered—also known as URL (Unique Resource Locator) in technical terms. Among the millions of websites, your domain helps prospective clients locate and contact you.

Unique identity Registration & hosting WHOIS & ICANN

First Step Online

The first step to make your online presence is to get a domain registration done. We at Taarruni help you explore the most suitable domain name out of the available domains. The domain name should be easily remembered and spelled. Sometimes even the simplest words are not available or are already registered—so it requires a lot of brainstorming to find the best possible domain.

ICANN Requirements

The basic requirements by ICANN to register a domain are your address, phone number, and e-mail ID. After completing the registration process, you can host your website on a reliable web server of your choice.

In the meantime your e-mail, phone number, and address get published in the public WHOIS database. We guide you through the process so your business is findable and professional online.

Hosting & Going Live

Once your domain is registered, we help you choose a reliable web server and host your website. A strong domain plus dependable hosting forms the foundation of your digital presence and helps clients locate and trust your business.
