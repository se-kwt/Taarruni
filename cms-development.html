Cms Web Development

Taarruni Design Solutions (TDS) is a leading digital marketing and IT solutions company.

What we deliver

The Content Management System (CMS) workflow we develop can reduce your website maintenance cost and efficiently let you make, deploy, and maintain content. By using the CMS, you can update and change items like news, price changes, and press releases without extra cost. We provide the best administrative tools for managing your content. The updation and relevancy of content—its freshness—are taken care of with flexible, adaptable systems. For e-commerce sites, CMS is a powerful solution that can boost your business.

Why CMS for Your Website

CMS are password protected and provide better security. At a single click you can update and modify your content in a protected way. It also enhances your employees’ productivity by saving time—you don’t need a programmer or HTML knowledge to update the content. Our developers create the most suitable CMS web applications for easy authoring and delivery of your content. We ensure flexibility and adaptability while keeping content freshness in mind.

WordPress Website Development

WordPress is a widely used content management system for blog publication and full-site development. The ease to adapt it, customize it, and maintain it adds to its appeal. Used by over 200 million websites, it is one of the most popular tools for website development after Joomla.

  • Simple designs, adaptable templates, and plug-in modules
  • Unrestricted, open-source—we modify and add dynamism and freshness
  • Websites on WordPress get more traffic and are considered innovative for website development

The professionals at Taarruni are well versed with WordPress and related tools to provide you state-of-the-art website development solutions.

Joomla Website Development

Joomla is one of the most recognized Content Management Systems. We use it widely for developing websites and adding powerful applications to your pages. This freely available, open-source solution is user friendly and widely recommended. Our Joomla web development team can help you publish content on intranet and internet—ranging from online content, blogging, forum messages, classifieds, shopping, and e-commerce. The ease of use of Joomla-based websites is a key reason for its success.

  • Websites and web portals using Joomla; theme and template development
  • Joomla modules development and website maintenance
  • Remote creator modules for News, Articles, FAQs and Links; module integration
  • Email-a-friend and print-any-format for stories and articles
  • Components development; e-commerce websites development
  • Runs on any server (Solaris, Linux, FreeBSD, AIX)
  • Image library, object hierarchy, polls/surveys; in-line text editor; template manager, custom modules
